# 🤖 Claude Notebook

Welcome to your Notebook home. Open any note, then use the prompt bar below to **chat**, switch to **edit** mode to have Claude revise the note directly, or **quiz** mode to test yourself.

Try one of these to see it work:

- Open **[[Elasticity of Demand]]** and ask: *"quiz me on this note."*
- Open **[[Hypothesis Testing]]** and ask: *"summarise the key formulas as a table."*
- Drag a PDF or Word file anywhere into Obsidian and watch it get converted and filed automatically.

Everything Claude says is grounded in *your* notes — it reads them before answering.
